# GoBrik 3.1

GoBrik is the ecological action platform developed and maintained by the Global Ecobrick Alliance.

This is the third major version of our app and represents our full transition from the Knack.com database platform to our own self‑hosted MySQL infrastructure. GoBrik 3.1 integrates seamlessly with ecobricks.org and our Buwana user credential system.

This release also marks a repository relaunch to remove accidentally published API keys and reorganize our server directory structure.

## About GoBrik

GoBrik is a social action platform providing ecobrickers and their communities around the world with tools to log, track, exchange, and manage their ecobricking.

GoBrik is a project of the Global Ecobrick Alliance (GEA) Earth Enterprise. Launched in 2017, GoBrik now connects over 50,000 users and 5,000 communities across 120 countries.

At the core of the platform is our global community of planet‑passionate ecobrickers. Through their hard work, our ecobrick authentication system generates the Brikcoin manual blockchain.

The GoBrik platform and its development are managed by the Global Ecobrick Alliance.

## Purpose

The GoBrik platform supports both the technological and philosophical infrastructure of the plastic transition movement.

GoBrik helps maintain clarity of the mandalic core pattern and intention of the ecobrick movement. The platform promotes shared terminology, best practices, and principles established by continental ecobrick leaders and reflected in the official Wikipedia ecobrick page.

As an independent Earth Enterprise that does not accept government or corporate funding, we strive to make our work open source and to use platforms and services that are likewise open source.

GoBrik enables us to support and amplify the work of our 327 Trainers around the world, whose regular contributions help cover maintenance and development costs.

Teams of trainers collaborate on development, translation, content creation, and conceptual evolution of the platform.
